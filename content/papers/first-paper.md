ArcheNova begins not with prediction, but with initial conditions.

An initial condition is not merely a starting point. It is a constraint that determines which futures remain reachable and which futures disappear forever.

Civilization is not shaped only by ideas, technologies, or policies. It is shaped by the conditions under which choices become irreversible.

This paper examines irreversible initial conditions as the foundation of civilization design.
